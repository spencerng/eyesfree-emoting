Method 2: Scroll Wheel
You will be given 6 emoticons to send without looking at your screen.
  1. Upon audio cue, select the emoticon using the scroll wheel without looking at the screen.
  2. Your phone will vibrate to indicate the emoticon was received. Select next emoticon upon audio cue.
  3. A long vibration will indicate that you are finished and can look at screen.
